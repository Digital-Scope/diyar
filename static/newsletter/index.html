Souq Al Baraha set to open 2021
Souq Al Baraha is set to become the newest and most distinctive destination for family shopping and entertainment in the Kingdom of Bahrain. It is located in the heart of Diyar Al Muharraq, centered amongst its residential communities. Souq Al Baraha boasts a total land area of approximately 64,202 sqm, and a total built up area of over 20,000 sqm. It also consists of leasable areas spanning over 13,000 sqm and includes approximately 468 commercial shops. Souq Al Baraha also offers 20 leasable office spaces, and approximately 50 kiosks and designated ATM spaces. Additionally, Souq Al Baraha visitors will be able to park their vehicles in a designated parking space designed to accommodate 686 vehicles. Its distinctive design will reflect on the unique architecture and culture of the Kingdom of Bahrain. Work on the main foundations started in December 2019, Souq Al Baraha is set to be completed by Q1 of 2021, and doors will be open to the general public by Q2 of the same year. Souq Al Baraha will include shops selling food and fresh produce, such as the fish market, fruits and vegetables market, meat market, household goods, in addition to tailors, fabric shops, and others that sell jewelry, gold and other Bahraini handicrafts. The market will also include a number of cafés and restaurants, an entertainment center, fuel station, mosque, supermarket, and other public facilities.

Diyar Al Muharraq Infrastructure is
Sustainable and Designed to High Standards

Diyar Al Muharraq Infrastructure is
Sustainable and Designed to High Standards
The infrastructure built in Diyar Al Muharraq is a state of the art model in sustainability, inspiring a harmony between aesthetic design and environmental responsibility.

In efforts to reduce the carbon footprint, local rock was used where possible in the reclamation process, decreasing the carbon emitted through shipping and trucking. In terms of energy preservation, Diyar has installed a modern irrigation network incorporating sustainable solar panels to provide power to some of the automated valves throughout the development network. Further sustainability is achieved as all the individual asset irrigation tanks can communicate with the main central holding reservoir so that water is pumped in the system on demand, rather than on a timer, making water usage more efficient.

Meanwhile, new technologies are incorporated into traditional engineering. Diyar’s Deep Gravity Sewer has tunnels in excess of 20 meters deep. Diyar Al Muharraq has also installed a number of strategic stormwater outfalls throughout the city, in order to provide an efficient rainwater disposal network throughout the city. Both networks are fully non-mechanical relying on gravity as the key engineering ethos.

DEEP GRAVITY FOUL SEWER NETWORK IS COMPLETED
On 31 March 2020, phase 1 of the Deep Gravity Foul Sewer Network became operational. By April, 30, 2020, phase 2, and the final phase of the South Island network became fully operational, with a capacity to serve the entire Island’s population.

The network comprising a 25 m deep underground lift station with an integral 340 m3 sewage reservoir, located by the entrance of the City, collects all the sewage from the development via a 6.4 km deep gravity network. The lift pump then automatically pumps the sewage over 6 kms to Hidd intermediate lift station for further treatment at Muharraq Sewage Treatment Plant, thereby providing the development with a most modern and very efficient sewerage removal system for the benefit of the Diyar Al Muharraq residents.

Diyar Al Muharraq Announces Al Bareh Project Updates
Diyar Al Muharraq announces the completion of all primary and secondary infrastructure works on Al Bareh villas, located on the Northern area of the South Island of Diyar Al Muharraq.

Infrastructure works were contracted by Dadabhai Construction, commenced mid February 2019. Diyar Al Muharraq Sponsors the First Bahraini Team Participating in Dakar Rally in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia
Diyar Al Muharraq sponsored the Bahraini team participating in Dakar Rally 2020, organized through Wheels of Arabia, from 5th to the 17th of January, in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Diyar Al Muharraq’s sponsorship is part of its contribution and efforts towards national responsibility. The Dakar Rally was held for the first time in KSA this year, with Bahraini rider Salman Mohammed as the first ever participant from the Kingdom of Bahrain.

Dakar Rally is an anticipated annual event with a wide audience that spans across the globe, with competitors participating from over 20 countries. The race classifies as an endurance race, with different types of off-road vehicles and bikes modified to meet the requirements, competing against each other, facing the most difficult rugged terrain, throughout various routes, ranging from mud, rock, sand tracks and more.
