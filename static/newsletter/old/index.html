Welcome Note

We also offer unique opportunities for residential and commercial projects, including commercial villa plots, commercial showroom plots, and warehousing plots.

I am pleased to introduce the first edition of the Diyar Al Muharraq newsletter called Diyarna, which will help to keep everyone up to date with our latest news and exciting project milestones.

Diyar Al Muharraq is one of the largest real estate developments in the Kingdom of Bahrain. It is a fully-integrated modern city best described as a complete society, incorporating a strong line-up of residential projects such as Hai Al Noor, Hai Al Sherooq, Al Bareh, Al Naseem, Jeewan, Deerat Al Oyoun, to name a few. While each project has a distinct appeal, they are all ideal for individuals and families with different needs.

Accordingly, we also offer unique opportunities for residential and commercial projects, including commercial villa plots, commercial showroom plots, and warehousing plots. The plots are strategically located around Diyar Al Muharraq, which will help to attract a greater number of business owners, investors and shoppers.

This December is set to be a landmark month. We look forward to opening Hadiqat Al Diyar, the largest park in Diyar Al Muharraq’s masterplan. In addition, special cycling and walking paths will be completed by the end of this year. Meanwhile, we are proud of our continued progress in implementing the masterplan, which is moving on schedule.

Work on the landscaping, the deep gravity foul sewer network and the landscaping irrigation network including the irrigation holding tank has gained momentum and is almost complete. The Water Distribution Station is completed and is now being operated by EWA. The vast majority of the main roads in the South Island are now also completed with the final roads in the South Island being completed by mid-2021. Additionally, the South Island master plan remaining infrastructure will be approximately 80% completed by the end of the first quarter of 2020.

On behalf of Diyar Al Muharraq, I would like to thank you all for your continuous support and trust, which are vital for the company to achieve its plans and goals. You can keep apprised on all the latest details from our projects by subscribing to our newsletter.

Wishing you a pleasant read.

Best regards,

Eng. Ahmed Alammadi
Chief Executive Officer

Hadiqat Al Diyar Park set to open later this year
Hadiqat Al Diyar, the largest park in Diyar Al Muharraq’s masterplan is set for completion in the fourth quarter of this year. With lush green spaces and landscaping, it will offer a range of amenities for families and individuals.
Located at the main entrance next to Shaikh Isa Bin Salman Al Khalifa Grand Mosque, the 32,800 SQM park is set to be one of Diyar Al Muharraq’s most popular attractions, supporting the developer’s vision to offer a contemporary and enjoyable lifestyle within the fully integrated, modern city.

Hadiqat Al Diyar features more than 200 parking spaces, two football pitches, one multi-purpose hall, and a children’s play area covering almost 400 SQM. It will also offer a running track, two outdoor gyms, a children’s playground, two large picnic spaces, and a food & beverage area.

Marassi Shores Residences Handover
Handover has begun for the eagerly anticipated Marassi Shores Residences, a key project within the Marassi Al Bahrain development. Offering contemporary living spaces and panoramic sea views, the project is set to be one of the most desirable addresses in the Kingdom.
Spread across 11 floors, Marassi Shores Residences is comprised of 289 apartments offered in studio and 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5-bedroom configurations. Residents can also make the most of its exceptional amenities, including an on-site gym, two swimming pools, children’s play areas, basement parking, and 24-hour security.

Idyllically situated on the waterfront promenade, it is within walking distance of two world-class hotels, Address Marassi Al Bahrain and Vida Marassi Al Bahrain, as well as a range of shopping, dinning, entertainment, and leisure destinations.

ThaiMart the region’s first Thai-inspired shopping destination
ThaiMart, the region’s first Thai-inspired shopping destination, will offer an exceptional variety of Thai products and services under one roof, further bolstering the strong relationship between the two countries.
The project is gaining momentum, with most of its retail space already leased and committed, and construction work is handled by Crown Electro – Mechanical Services. Vega Intertrade and Exhibitions will lease and operate the eagerly anticipated shopping destination.

Spread across 6,000 SQM in the southwest corner of Diyar Al Muharraq, ThaiMart will offer a range of sought after products exclusively imported from Thailand. With its convenient highway access, growing residential and commercial community, and businessfriendly environment, Diyar Al Muharraq is an ideal location for the project.

In addition to jewelry zones, a kids’ area, and a wide selection of retail outlets, ThaiMart will feature a food court housing well-known Thai, international, and regional restaurant chains, a fish market, a vegetable market, and a gold market.
Masterplan comes together
Designed as a sustainable future city, Diyar Al Muharraq is one of the largest mixed-use real estate development projects in Bahrain.
Upon completion, it will feature a cohesive mix of residential and commercial properties with a strong line-up of projects aimed at creating a vibrant community within a sophisticated township.

Tying the development’s various projects together is an ambitious masterplan, which will streamline traffic flow throughout the development by linking its various sections via a network of roads, as well as pedestrian paths and bicycle tracks, which are scheduled to be completed by the end of this year.

Key updates on the project include the completion of the main roads on Diyar Al Muharraq’s Southern Island and the Water Distribution Station which holds 11 million gallons of water. Additionally, the majority of the secondary road network servicing the villas and plots in the Southern Island are also completed. Momentum is also gaining for the landscaping and irrigation network projects throughout the Southern Island which will be completed by end of 2019. Roads in Al Qamra, Sarat, Souq Al Baraha and most of the roads in Business District are completed. Works on secondary roads in Hai Al Noor, Hai Al Sherooq and Phase 1 Al Bareh are completed.

Eight primary 66KV substations, along with 159 11KV substations are presently operational. Planning with EWA for the installation of the 220KV substation that will serve the North Island has begun. Fiber optic containment on all existing roads is finalized. Diyar Al Muharraq also announced the completion of infrastructure work on its key projects: Deerat Al Oyoun, Al Qamra, and Al Bareh.
